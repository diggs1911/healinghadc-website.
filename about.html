About Us

Healing H.A.D.C., Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit committed to compassionate, professional care across Galveston County and beyond.

Under our DBA “Caring Hearts Independent Living,” we offer home health, behavioral health, independent living, and community services to empower individuals in all life stages.

Our Mission

To foster independence and well-being—one heart, one mind, one community at a time.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Rooted in empathy at every turn.
  • Excellence: Surpassing standards through innovation.
  • Integrity: Transparency and trust in all we do.
  • Collaboration: Partnerships that magnify our impact.
