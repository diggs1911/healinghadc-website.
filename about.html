Healing H.A.D.C., Inc. is a compassionate and dedicated 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to positively transforming lives throughout Galveston County and the surrounding regions. We offer an integrated approach to care, providing essential services in home health, behavioral health counseling, independent living solutions, and proactive community outreach. Our dedicated team focuses on empowering individuals, supporting families, and strengthening community bonds. We aim to create environments where both our clients and our team members feel valued, supported, and inspired.

Our Mission is to foster independence, promote wellness, and enrich lives—one heart, one mind, and one community at a time. We are passionately dedicated to delivering personalized, compassionate care that honors dignity, respects individual needs, and enhances the overall quality of life.

Compassion: Empathy and understanding shape every service we provide, ensuring genuine care and kindness.

Excellence: Driven by innovation and ongoing improvement, we consistently strive to surpass expectations and set new standards in care.

Integrity: Honesty, transparency, and accountability are fundamental to everything we do, building trust with our clients, staff, and partners.

Collaboration: We actively seek partnerships with families, healthcare providers, and community organizations, understanding that collective efforts lead to stronger, healthier, and more resilient communities.

Join us at Healing H.A.D.C., Inc. and become part of a compassionate team dedicated to making a meaningful difference. Together, let's inspire wellness, independence, and community strength.
