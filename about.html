About Our Founder, Mary Williams

In 1985, Mary Ann Williams graduated with a B.A. in Business Administration. Driven by faith and community, she founded Choo Choo Train Christian Academy in 1995 with just five children in her home, later expanding to six buildings serving over 300 students.

Answering God’s call, she transitioned to adult care—bringing the same compassion to Healing HADC. Today our 24/7 care facility carries her legacy of love and service.
