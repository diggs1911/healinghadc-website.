About Healing H.A.D.C., Inc.

Healing H.A.D.C., Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to providing compassionate, professional care across the lifespan. Founded as a faith-based outreach, we partner with Medicare, HHS and Social Security to deliver outcomes-driven home health, behavioral health, independent living, and community services throughout Galveston County and beyond.

Our Mission

To empower independence and foster wellbeing—one heart, one mind, one community at a time.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Every interaction is rooted in empathy and respect.
  • Excellence: We exceed standards through ongoing training and innovation.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, accountability, and trust.
  • Collaboration: Strong partnerships magnify our community impact.
