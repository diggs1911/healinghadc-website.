Meet Our Founder

Mary Ann Williams

Her Journey of Faith & Service

In 1985, Mary Ann Williams graduated with a B.A. in Business Administration. Fueled by her passion for church, children and community, she founded Choo Choo Train Christian Academy in 1995 with five students in her home. Under her leadership, it expanded to six buildings, serving 300+ children with a charter school, youth camp and Head Start program.

Answering God’s call to adult care, she established Healing H.A.D.C.—bringing the same compassion to adults in need. Today, our 24/7 facility continues her legacy of dignity, respect and holistic support.

Empowering independence, one heart at a time.
