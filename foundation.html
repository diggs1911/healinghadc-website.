Mary Ja Ne Williams

About the Founder

In 1985, Mary Ja Ne Williams earned her Bachelor of Arts degree in Business Administration, laying a solid foundation for her life’s mission of service. Guided by unwavering faith and a deep-seated commitment to her community, she founded Choo Choo Train Christian Academy in 1995. What began humbly with just five children blossomed under her dedicated leadership into a vibrant educational community serving over 300 students, along with the establishment of a successful charter school, youth camp, and impactful Head Start program.

Mary’s profound dedication to uplifting those around her extended far beyond education. She actively engaged in numerous community initiatives, becoming a recognized leader and advocate for families, youth, and seniors alike. Her compassionate spirit inspired her next significant endeavor—transitioning into adult care. Answering a divine call, she established Healing H.A.D.C., Inc., a comprehensive 24/7 adult care facility rooted in the same compassion, empathy, and devotion that characterized her educational ventures.

Healing H.A.D.C., Inc. now stands as a lasting testament to Mary’s tireless service and enduring legacy of love. It continues to embody her lifelong commitment to dignity, care, and holistic community well-being.
