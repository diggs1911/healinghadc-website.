Mary Williams Foundation for Hope & Healing

In 1985, Mary Ann Williams graduated with a B.A. in Business Administration. Motivated by her faith and love for community, she founded Choo Choo Train Christian Academy in 1995—growing it from 5 children to 300+, plus a charter school, youth camp, and Head Start program.

Answering God’s call, she transitioned to adult care—with the same compassion she’d shown children—launching Healing H.A.D.C., Inc. Her 24/7 care facility continues her legacy of love and service.

Core Programs

  • Legacy Scholarships: Renewable awards for students in healthcare, social work, or behavioral health.
  • Hope & Healing Summer Institute: 6-week camp on mental-health literacy, cancer prevention, nutrition & fitness.
  • Hearts of Hope Banquet: Annual gala honoring partners and scholarship winners.
