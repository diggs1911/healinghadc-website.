Mary Williams Foundation for Hope & Healing

Investing in Our Community’s Future

The Mary Williams Foundation raises and allocates funds to uplift underserved families, seniors and the next generation of healthcare professionals.

Signature Initiatives

  • Mary Williams Legacy Scholarships — renewable awards for students pursuing mental-health, social-work & healthcare degrees.
  • Hope & Healing Summer Institute — 6-week camp on mental health, cancer prevention, nutrition & fitness.
  • Hearts of Hope Banquet — annual gala honoring partners, announcing scholars & funding next year’s grants.
