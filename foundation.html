Mary Williams Foundation for Hope & Healing

In 1985, Mary Ann Williams earned her B.A. in Business Administration, then founded Choo Choo Train Christian Academy in her home—growing it to six buildings, 300+ children, a charter school, youth camp and Head Start program.

Answering God’s call, she transitioned to adult care—bringing that same compassion to Healing H.A.D.C., Inc., where her legacy of service continues in our 24/7 care facility.

Core Programs

  • Legacy Scholarships: Renewable awards for students in healthcare, social work or behavioral health (5×$3k in Year 1).
  • Hope & Healing Summer Institute: 6-week camp on mental health literacy, cancer prevention, nutrition & fitness.
  • Hearts of Hope Banquet: Annual gala honoring partners, announcing scholars & presenting the Community Healing Award.

3-Year Growth Plan

  1. Year 1: Pilot scholarships & one-day health bootcamp.
  2. Year 2: Full 6-week institute & double scholarship awards.
  3. Year 3: Corporate underwriting, livestream banquet, endowment fund for 15+ scholarships annually.
