About Healing H.A.D.C.

Healing H.A.D.C., Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to restoring independence through professional home health, behavioral health services, independent living, and community outreach in Galveston County and beyond.

Operating under our DBAs, our mission is to uplift families, seniors, and underserved communities with compassionate care. We believe every individual deserves dignity—and that’s the heart of everything we do.

Meet Our Founder

In 1985, Mary Ann Williams earned her B.A. in Business Administration, then founded Choo Choo Train Christian Academy in 1995—growing from five children in her home to six buildings serving 300+ youth. Called by God to adult care, she now champions Healing H.A.D.C., dedicating her life to adult compassion & excellence.
