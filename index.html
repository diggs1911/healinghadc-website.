About Us

At Healing H.A.D.C., INC, we provide services that promote independence, well-being, and support for individuals in our community. Operating under our DBA, Caring Hearts Independent Living, our mission is to nurture each person’s unique needs with empathy, respect, and professionalism.

Our experienced team works tirelessly to ensure our clients feel respected and cared for, offering programs that help them maintain dignity and autonomy in their daily lives.

Our Services

We offer a range of programs and resources designed to foster independent living, encourage social interaction, and improve overall wellness.

In-Home Care

Personalized support for daily activities, ensuring a safe and comfortable living environment.

Wellness & Social Programs

Engaging group activities, exercise routines, and wellness workshops to enhance mental and physical health.

Counseling & Support

Professional guidance and emotional support for individuals and families adjusting to new life stages.

Nutrition & Meal Planning

Healthy meal programs and dietary counseling tailored to individual health requirements.
